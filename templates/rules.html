Stock Screener Scoring

N-Value Rating

To calculate this score, each rule below is given a n-value from 1-8. Then if the stock passes that rule it receives a score of 2^n.

Lwowski Rating

To calculate this score, the percentage difference between the actual rule's value and the passing threshold value is calculated. Then the percent difference is multiplied by the n-value score above.

Stock Screener Rules

50d MA greater than 150d MA Rule

Summary: For stocks to pass SMA50 > SMA150
N-Value: 12

150d MA greater than 200d MA Rule

Summary: For stocks to pass SMA150 > SMA200
N-Value: 11

52 week high low span Rule

Summary: For stocks to pass 0.75*week52_high > 1.25*week52_low
N-Value: 10

Relative Strength Greater Than 1.0

Summary: For stocks to pass the EMA60 of ((curr_price-prev_price)/prev_price) / ((curr_price_s&p500-prev_price_s&p500)/prev_price_s&p500) has to be greater than 1.0
N-Value: 9

Liquidity Rule

Summary: For stocks to pass SMA50_value*SMA50_volume <= 20 million
N-Value: 8

Close above 52 week high - 25% Rule

Summary: For stocks to pass prev_close > 0.75*week52_high
N-Value: 7

Previous Close Greater Than $10

Summary: To pass the previous close of the stock has to be greater than $10.
N-Value: 6

Positive 200d SMA Rule

Summary: For stocks to pass the 200d SMA current day has to be greater than the previous day for all of the last 21 days
N-Value: 5

Institutional Ownership Rule

Summary: For stocks to pass 5% <= inst_own
N-Value: 4

Close above 50d MA Rule

Summary: For stocks to pass prev_close > SMA50
N-Value: 3

Sales QoQ Yearly Rule

Summary: For stocks to pass Sales QoQ Yearly > 25%
N-Value: 2

EPS QoQ Yearly Rule

Summary: For stocks to pass EPS QoQ Yearly > 18%
N-Value: 1
